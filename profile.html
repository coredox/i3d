About Us

INNOVATIVE 3D SOLUTIONS was established in the year 2010. The company has all the ingredients which make an organization successful and commands the domain of computer aided design services. The company has hands set on CAD/CAM engineering, plastic molding services, 3D scanning services, technical engineering services and products design services and so on and so forth. We are reckoned as one of the fewest experts of tool design service in India. We have a habit of timely delivery of the service because customer relationships are very pivotal for us.

We have a team of engineers who are working on different software in the field of reverse engineering (Class A & Class B) & CAD Like CATIA, Imageware, UG NX, Solid Works etc. In our infrastructure, we have Blue Light Scanner from STEINBICHLER with an accuracy of 10 microns per meter. Additionally, Photogrammetry System for greater accuracy is also available.

We proffer general and customized services, whatever may be the case. With an output-oriented methodology of Identification of the Client's Requirements, Concept design, Mock up & Prototype Generation, we cater the requisites of our clientele with great ease. Fine finishing and accuracy in work keeps our customers pouring in again and again. The virtuoso workforce is our asset helping us to emerge as a flourishing entity.
